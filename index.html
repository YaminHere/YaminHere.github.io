About me

Hey! I’m a Full-Stack Designer specializing in product development, UI/UX, branding, and graphics. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive designs.

I combine artistry and technological know-how to create user-focused solutions that are aesthetically appealing as well as practical. I have a magic in Adobe Creative Suite, figma and some other tools which help me to develop coherent designs from idea to final product. Working with cross-functional teams to work out design concepts is also something I enjoy doing.
